# Module 1: Introduction to Physical AI and Embodied Intelligence

This module introduces the fundamental concepts of Physical AI and embodied intelligence that form the foundation of humanoid robotics.

## Overview

Physical AI represents a paradigm shift from traditional AI systems that operate in virtual spaces to AI systems that interact with and understand the physical world. This module explores how embodiment influences intelligence and the current state of humanoid robotics.

## Learning Objectives

- Understand the principles of Physical AI and embodied intelligence
- Recognize the importance of embodiment in artificial intelligence
- Survey the current landscape of humanoid robotics
